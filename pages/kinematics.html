Kinematics

What is Kinematics?

In robotics, the problem of finding out how a robot moves regardless of the forces behind the aforementioned movement is called kinematics. We can talk about two main types of kinematic problems:

  • Forward kinematics: Where we compute the unknown position and orientation of a robot given some joint movements.
  • Inverse kinematics: Where we an unknown a combination of joint movements that would make the robot move to a desired position and orientation.
